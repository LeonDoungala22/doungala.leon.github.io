About Me

I am Leon Doungala, an AI/ML Engineer and Data Scientist with a strong focus on solving real-world challenges using artificial intelligence. My academic background includes an MSc in Computer Engineering and Automation, with my thesis, "AI and ML Techniques for Diabetes Prediction and Characterization," showcasing my dedication to healthcare innovation through AI and predictive modeling.

In 2024, as part of my professional experience, I developed an advanced and powerful AI-driven Applicant Tracking System (ATS). The GitHub version of this project serves as a minimal viable product (MVP) demo, similar to many of my other projects on GitHub. This system enhances candidate matching for HR teams, demonstrating my ability to design impactful AI solutions for business needs.

Additionally, my work on Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) AI systems has had a transformative impact in the healthcare sector. These systems are used by doctors in numerous clinics and have processed more than 150,000 unstructured referrals, delivering actionable insights and improving decision-making.

While my expertise extends across industries, I am deeply committed to healthcare. I focus on creating AI tools that support medical professionals and enhance patient care, leveraging advanced technologies to make a meaningful difference.
